OUR PRODUCTS

The Sacco products are classified into the Back Office Service Activity(B.O.S.A) and Front Office Service Activity (F.O.S.A). BOSA enable members to make non-withdrawable share deposits and borrow against these savings at competitive and affordable interest rates. FOSA products enable members to borrow against their savings without guarantors but with collateral security.
